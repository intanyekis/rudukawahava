Matt Rife bringing comedy tour to Charlotte WSOC TV

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 17, 2024

CHARLOTTE — Popular comedian Matt Rife is bringing his “ProbleMATTic World Tour” to the Carolinas later this year.

Rife, known for his viral social media content, will perform two shows at Ovens Auditorium in Charlotte on Dec. 10, according to a post on the Live Nation Carolinas Facebook page. Show times are 5 p.m. and 8 p.m.

The show is being billed “for mature audiences,” age 21 and older.

Tickets go on sale Friday at 10 a.m. through Ticketmaster. Click here for more information.

Other dates in the area include Oct. 26-27 at the Durham Performing Arts Center and Dec. 8 at the Township Auditorium in Columbia.
