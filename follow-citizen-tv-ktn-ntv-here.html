Here's How to Still Access Citizen TV, KTN and NTV After Switch Off

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

The Communications Authority on Tuesday morning switched off a number of television stations; Citizen TV and radio, Inooro TV, KTN and NTV.

It is not clear why CA officials switched off Citizen TV’s ADN signals at the Limuru station, Managing Director, Wachira Waruru said but assured Kenyans that they were working on resuming normal transmission shortly.

NTV and KTN’s free to air platforms were also switched off but remained uninterrupted on DSTV.

The stations continue their live coverage of the Raila Odinga swearing in ceremony set to take place anytime before 2pm.

Here is how you can follow the live coverage of events:

NTV: https://t.co/ioBlxPmB4h
KTN: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LhWpjkh0zv4
Citizen: https://t.co/Qoq6Bk8Mzj

The government has been accused of threatening media houses that intend to air live the controversial ‘oath’ taking ceremony.

Kenyans online have said that the government, led by President Uhuru Kenyatta is leading the country back to the dark days of dictatorship.
