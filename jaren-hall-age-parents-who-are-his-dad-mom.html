Jaren Hall Age Parents: Who Are His Dad Mom?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Jaren Hall has emerged out as a praiseworthy quarterback of BYU at the age of 21. Here’s more into his personal life details!

Jaren Hall is an American football quarterback.

He is predominantly known for his gameplay with Brigham Young University where he is enrolled in both football and baseball.

Hall has gained eminence via his football games and has evolved as one of the famous quarterbacks of the team.

Though he suffered from injuries in his debut game, he created history as the first African-American player to start as a quarterback of the BYU Cougars.

Jaren Hall Age And Birthday Revealed

Jaren Hall’s current age is revealed to be 21 via our reports.

However, the exact particulars of his birthday are not accessible at the moment.

Hall was born in Spanish Fork, Utah, and has resided in the state throughout his life.

Who Are Jaren Hall Parents?

Jaren Hall was born to his parents; Kalin Hall and Hollie Hamilton Hall. Both his parents are former athletes having played for BYU themselves.

His father Kalin played as a quarterback for BYU back in the 90s. Kalin is also a former JUCO Player of the Year at Dixie State in 1991.

He has been cited as a talented running back for BYU during the 1992 and 1993 seasons. Meanwhile, Jaren’s mother Hollie is a former gymnast of BYU.

Meet Jaren Hall Family

Jaren Hall hails from an athletic family background where his parents and siblings played for BYU.

In addition to his parents, his elder brother KJ was a running back at BYU. His other siblings include two younger brothers, Dawson and Kyson, and a younger sister, Mateya.

It is sure that the younger Hall siblings will also follow in the family’s footsteps of playing for BYU.

Likewise, Jaren has also started a family of his own at a young age. He is a loyal husband to Breanna McCarter and a loving father to their daughter, Jayda Mae Hall.

His wife Breanna is also a former Utah Valley soccer player and the athletic couple tied the knot in 2019.

Follow Jaren Hall On Instagram

The young Hall can be traced down on Instagram under the username @jt_hall1 where he has garnered more than 4.2k followers.

He has not shared much on the platform but he has given insights into his new family life.

The BYU star has dedicated most of his Instagram posts to his wife and his new family.
