DJ Uiagalelei Arm Tattoo Design And Meaning; More On His Family Ethnicity And Religion

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 18, 2024

DJ Uiagalelei arm tattoo is so attractive that the public cannot take their eyes off it.

Mr. Uiagalelei is an aspiring footballer based in the United States. He started playing football early and is now paving a path toward National Football League (NFL). 

Currently, he serves as a quarterback for his college team, Clemson Tigers, at Clemson University. He signed the college contract in 2020. Before that, DJ was a quarterback at St. John Basco High School. 

Moreover, the young footballer has made headlines after Clemson Tigers won the recent match against Georgia Tech on 5th September 2022. Though his team won, some news portals report that Uiagalelei had a shaky start to the game.

Nonetheless, DJ’s career stats are pretty impressive, and if everything goes well, we will see him as an NFL-drafted quarterback in some years.

DJ Uiagalelei Tattoo On Arm: Design And Meaning Explored

DJ Uiagalelei tattoo appears pretty big on his arm.

His tattoo starts right from his right wrist and extends to his elbow, i.e., his tattoo covers his entire arm. Now talking about its design and meaning, he has inked his last name, “Uiagalelei,” in Polynesian tribal patterns and has complemented its design with additional Samoan symbols.

Ever since the quarterback inked his right arm, his fans cannot stop talking about it. One of his fans posted a closeup picture of his tattoo and captioned it as “Clemson commit DJ Uiagalelei with one of the best tattoos I’ve seen.”

DJ Uiagalelei Family: Ethnicity And Religion

The quarterback was born in a family of Samoan ethnicity, and they are Christians.

Samoans are the indigenous people from Samoan Island, Polynesia. And DJ’s parents are first-generation Americans whose parents are Samoan Islanders. So, technically, DJ is only the 3rd generation of his tribe living in America.

Additionally, he was born on 17th April 2001 to his parents, Dave Uiagalelei (father) and Tausha Raquel (mother). His full name is David John Uiagalelei, and he is 21 years old. Likewise, the quarterback is the eldest son of his parents and has a younger brother, Matayo Uiagalelei.

According to the source, DJ’s father was the bodyguard of different celebrities like Rihana but later became a school resource officer to spend time with his family. Similarly, his mother was a full-time worker and even became a surrogate mother to provide for the family.

Hence, the Uiagalelei family struggled a lot to have a good life for the family. However, the parent’s hard work is fruitful as DJ becomes an exceptional football player. And their younger son Matayo is no less; he also plays football at school.

Previously, the parents never missed their sons’ practice, and now, they never forget their big match. They are always cheering from DJ and Matayo from the crowd.

Does DJ Uiagalelei Have Girlfriend

Yes, DJ Uiagalelei has a girlfriend. She is Ava Pritchard.

David and Ava have been dating since 2020. Both of them attend Clemson University and must have initially interacted at college. Likewise, Ava is an athlete like her boyfriend, but she plays college Volleyball.

The couple has made their relationship public, but the quarterback rarely posts about Ava on his social media. The only post he made was wishing her a happy birthday. However, Ava, on the other hand, is pretty vocal and often posts cute couple pictures.

Ava’s latest post with DJ was made in June, where the pair looked relaxed in a matching white outfit as DJ walked on with Ava on his back. Ava captioned the photo as “my best friend.”
