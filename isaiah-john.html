Isaiah John (born November 16, 1995) is an American Actor and Social Media Personality from Atlanta, Georgia. His career’s breakthrough role came with a TV series “Snowfall”. It is an American crime drama television series.

The creators of the show are Dave Andron, John Singleton, and Eric Amadio. Furthermore, John portrayed the main character named Leon Simmons who is Franklin’s best friend. The first episode of the series aired on FX on July 5, 2017.

Some of the other main casts of Snowfall are Damson Idris as Franklin Saint, Carter Hudson as Teddy McDonald, Emily Rios as Lucia Villanueva, Amin Joseph as Jerome Saint, Angela Lewis as Aunt Louie, Michael Hyatt as Cissy Saint, and much more.

Career, TV Shows & Movies

The talented actor made his acting debut with a 2016 comedy/drama movie called “Barbershop: The Next Cut”. It was released on April 15, 2016, and Malcolm D. Lee worked as a director of the movie.

Isaiah got an opportunity to portray one of the minor parts as a gang member. Later, in 2017, Isaiah then made his television debut with the show “Snowfall”. The second season of the series came out on July 19, 2018.

On July 10, 2019, its third season came out. As per the source, Snowfall season 4 will be premiered in 2020. John also worked in the TV movie titled “Downsized” directed by Rhonda Baraka.

The writer of the television movie was Michelle McKissick. He performed the role of Mike Jr. The other main casts of Downsized are Nicole Ari Parker as Ebony, Boris Kodjoe as Michael Sr., Alix Lapri as Madison, Racquel Bianca John as Michelle, etc.
