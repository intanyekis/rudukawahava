Lea Michele Gets Naked, Reveals Finn Tattoo for 'Women's Health UK': Pics

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

No laundry day for her. Lea Michele stripped down and went completely naked for the September 2016 issue of Women’s Health UK.

In the spread, the Scream Queens actress, 29, strategically covered up her assets with her hands and legs and kept her luscious brunette tresses down. She showed off several of her 14 tattoos, including the “Finn” ink she got in honor of her late boyfriend, Cory Monteith. The Canadian actor played Finn Hudson on Glee from 2009 to 2013.

Inside the mag, Michele opened up about her relationship goals. “I take care of myself, and I spend a lot of time and energy doing that, so I need someone else to look after themselves too,” she said. “It’s only then that you can come together and be a really good team.”

Last month, Us Weekly exclusively confirmed that Michele and actor Robert Buckley split after a few months of dating. “I have down days. I accept those days as much as the happy ones,” the Glee alum told the mag. “[When that happens I] make some macaroni cheese, call my mom, pour a glass of red wine and watch some reality television. There’s nothing that can’t fix.”

Michele keeps busy by exercising too. “Right now, I feel physically in my best shape, and emotionally in my best place,” she told Women’s Health UK. “I’m not perfect. I’m not trying to represent myself as being some perfect girl, but I love myself, flaws and all.”

Michele often posts photos on social media following her SoulCycle spin classes. “I’m not the kind of person who would go to the gym. For me, working out has to be good for my mind,” she explained. “When I leave, the endorphins are buzzing, I’m glowing and I feel like I can take on the world.”
