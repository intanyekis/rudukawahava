Who Is Dina Hashem?

She was born as Dina Hashem on August 25, 1989, in America to her Arabian parents whose names and information are not yet available to the media. It is still unknown if the young comedian is an only child or has any sibling (s), but we do know that she spent her childhood days in her hometown. She is a citizen of the US and she has an Arabic ancestry.

Digging into her school life, there are no details concerning the schools she attended in the early years of her life. However, we learned that she studied at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey (formerly known as Queen college), a public research institution, located at New Jersey. While she was studying there, Dina Hashem participated in the school’s comedy festival where she was crowned the winner and took home the winning price.

Ever since she was a child, Dina Hashem has always had a passion for comedy which saw to her appearing in one of the episodes of the Comedy Central American sketch comedy television series Inside Amy Schumer and also a web comical series Broad City. She later made her film debut as the led female character in the movie Ghost Friend as Willow in 2016.

In 2018, Hashem went ahead to compete in the Comedy Central Roast Battle, eventually, she made a guest appearance in an episode of the TBS late-night talk show CONAN anchored by a comedian and writer Conan O’Brien. Apart from being an amazing comedian, Dina Hashem is also a talented writer whose works has been used in TV Land Websites, Nickelodeon, and Comedy Central. Besides, she has made other noteworthy appearance in Sisters (2015), The Comedy Show Show (2015) and Urge (2016).
