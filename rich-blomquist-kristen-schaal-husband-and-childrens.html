Kristen Schaal Husband: Who Is Rich Blomquist?

Rich Blomquist is the husband of Kristen Schaal. He is an American writer, producer, and occasional actor. He is originally from Westbrook Connecticut

Blomquist was born on July 27, 1977. He graduated 2000 from the School of Communications at Elon University in North Carolina.

He is a prominent American writer of Swedish descent and has contributed to many talk shows.

Blomquist has been a staff writer for The Daily Show in 2003–2006, 2009, and 2011. He has also contributed material to Saturday Night Live, specifically for Robert Smigel’s TV Funhouse.

In addition to being a staff writer for talk shows, Blomquist has also written Earth (The Book) and The Sexy Book of Sexy Sex, co-written with his wife.

The husband of the comedian is also active on Twitter. As of July 2023, he has about 5k people following him on Twitter.
