What Everyone Forgets About Their Romance

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Kristin Cavallari is a mom of three. She will also soon be the ex-wife of former NFL player, Jay Cutler. She shares three kids with Jay. While juggling the roles of a wife and mom, Kristin wrote a New York Best Seller and two cookbooks. Kristin also owns and runs a jewelry company by the name Uncommon James while sparing time to design shoes.

Long before Kristin starred in the reality show, Very Cavallari, which broadcasts her life with her family, she had appeared in two other reality TV series, Laguna Beach and its spin-off, The Hills. People associate Kristin with Brody during her time on The Hills where she supposedly dated him. However and as we are about to see, not everything that appears on reality TV is real.

Kristin and Brody had an on and off TV relationship that lasted less than a year. Although some fans were rooting for the couple to end up together, Kristin and Brody’s romance fizzled when she left town during The Hills finale. Here are other details about their relationship.

13 Kristin Dated Brody Prior To Appearing With Him On TV

Kristin met Brody through one of her co-stars in Laguna Beach. Talan Torriero is the one who introduced Brody to Kristin. The two started dating after the show ended and their relationship lasted for almost a year. The ex-couple ended their on-again-off-again relationship in 2006 right about the time Kristin was 18 years.

12 Kristin And Brody’s TV Relationship Was Fake

The only time when Kristin and Brody were together as a real-life couple was the time they dated after Laguna Beach. The two met again while filming The Hills. They were requested by the producers of the show to pretend that they were dating one another. Their relationship lasted a few episodes.

11 Kristin Was Prepared To Fake It

Kristin is no longer being silent about her time on her first reality TV shows. She says that a lot of what was shown on the two reality TVs mentioned above was manipulated to fit a narrative the producers wanted to tell. Kristin was unsure about how to handle herself on Laguna Beach but when Kristin was cast on The Hills, she was prepared to say and do whatever her producers wanted her to do.

10 Kristin Lied About Her Relationship With Brody

Not only did Kristin pretend to be dating Brody in The Hills but she also led viewers to believe that this was the first mature relationship she had been in and it would have been believable were it true. Prior to dating and fake dating Brody, the only other serious relationship she had ever been in was with her high school boyfriend Stephen Colletti.

9 Kristin And Brody’s TV Relationship Was Never Solid

You would think that after going through all the trouble of making viewers believe that Kristin and Brody were dating, The Hills producers would at least allow them to have a solid relationship. This was not the case, instead, Kristin and Brody kept having these near misses. Kristin was even linked to other people in the show.

8 Kristin Was Involved With Another Co-Star

Brody was not the only love interest for Kristin on The Hills. Kristin was also involved with Justin Bobby who was Audrina Patridge’s on-again-off-again boyfriend. Kristin also tried to hook up with a bartender to make Brody jealous which backfired on her. Brody started to date another woman and this did not help the relationship in any way.

7 Brody Was In A Relationship With A Model While Supposedly Dating Kristin

Brody and Kristin did eventually date for a few episodes but since we know that their romance was not real, we wonder who their real partners at the time were if at all, they had any. They both did, Brody was in a relationship with a Playboy model by the name Jayde Nicole.

6 Kristin Dated One Of The Cameramen When She Was Meant To Be With Brody

Kristin, on the other hand, was involved with one of the cameramen filming the reality show. While Brody pretended that she had broken up with Nicole, Kristin was in a relationship with Miguel Medina but she had to keep this a secret otherwise it would blow her cover as Brody’s girlfriend. The two were busted by paparazzi a week before the filming of the show was to be completed.

5 Kristin Got Into A Staged Fight With Brody’s Real Girlfriend

Part of the ruse of getting viewers to believe that Kristin was in a relationship with Brody was to stage a fight between Nicole and Kristin to make it look like the two women were fighting over Brody. The squabble played out after Nicole saw Brody and Kristin at a club together.

4 The Ex-Couple Was Just Not Meant To Be

Kristin and Brody’s romance did not work out the time they dated in real life and the time they fake dated on TV. The two were just not meant to be. Kristin has been married to her soon to be ex-husband for a decade. Brody, on the other hand, married Kaitlynn Carter but the two split after a year of marriage.

3 Kristin And Brody Kept In Touch

Although things never worked out between Kristin and Brody, the ex-couple consider themselves friends. In one of the episodes in her recent show, Kristin is filmed catching up with her former co-stars. She reveals to them that she has been keeping in touch with Brody, the two talk but not regularly.

2 Brody Did Not Invite Kristin To His Wedding

Kristin was hoping to get an invite to Brody’s wedding especially after speaking to Brody a month prior to the event. When she did not get an invitation, she decided that she would also not send a gift to the couple. The June wedding took place on an island in Indonesia.

1 There Are No Hard Feelings Between Them, And She Approves Of Kaitlynn

There are no hard feelings between Kristin and Brody. Kristin may have been briefly upset for missing an invitation to Brody’s wedding but she is happy for Brody and his new wife. She approves of Kaitlynn and further goes on to confirm that Jenner’s mother, Linda Thompson also approves of the union.
