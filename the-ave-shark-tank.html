The Ave Shark Tank Founder – Nick Romero

Nick Romero is the visionary founder behind The Ave Venice, a company that has since rebranded as The Ave Los Angeles. With his passion for apparel and a keen eye for customization, Romero embarked on an entrepreneurial journey that would revolutionize the world of custom clothing and footwear.

In his quest to bring unique and personalized fashion to the masses, Romero acquired a shirt printing machine and dedicated two years to perfecting it. He also developed specialized software that allowed for bespoke artwork to be applied to sneakers and clothes. With his innovative approach to customization, Romero caught the attention of the Shark Tank panel and decided to seek an investment to take his business to new heights.

From Idea to Reality

What began as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business. Romero’s dedication and laser focus allowed him to establish The Ave Venice, a brand that embodied the spirit of individuality and self-expression. By harnessing the power of cutting-edge technology and artistic ingenuity, Romero laid the foundation for what would become a trailblazing company in the world of custom apparel.

“I wanted to create a brand that celebrated creativity and personal style. The Ave Venice is a reflection of my commitment to providing individuals with unique pieces of wearable art.”

Nick Romero’s appearance on Shark Tank showcased his unwavering entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to his craft. Seeking a $125,000 investment for 15% equity in The Ave Venice, Romero impressed the Sharks with his innovative designs and unwavering passion. Though a deal was not secured, Romero’s journey on Shark Tank was just the beginning of his remarkable success story.
