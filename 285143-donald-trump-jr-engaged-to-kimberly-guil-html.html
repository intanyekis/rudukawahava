Donald Trump Jr. Engaged To Kimberly Guilfoyle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Donald Trump Jr. | Getty Images

Donald Trump is in the news once again, but not in the way you’d think. This time it’s Donald Trump Jr., and he’s got some big news of his own.

Donald Trump’s son affectionately referred to as “Don,” has finally revealed that he has been engaged for a year.

Don proposed to his then-girlfriend, Kimberly Guilfoyle, on New Year’s Eve 2020, and the couple has been keeping the engagement under wraps for a year.

Usually, when a couple gets engaged, the first thing they want to do is tell anybody who will listen, but for Don and Kimberly, they wanted to keep their news a secret.

The couple decided to keep their engagement under wraps while settling into their Florida lives after moving from New York. Between the two, they have six children, who they said they wanted to focus on.

The news was revealed via a photo taken of the pair celebrating Don’s 44th birthday. Kimberly can be seen wearing an 8-carat diamond ring on her left ring finger.
