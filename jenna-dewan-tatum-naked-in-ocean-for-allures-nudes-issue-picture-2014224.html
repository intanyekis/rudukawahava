Jenna Dewan Tatum Naked in Ocean for Allure's &quot;Nudes&quot; Issue: Picture

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Water bed, for real! Jenna Dewan Tatum strips down completely naked in the May 2014 issue of Allure magazine for its long-awaited “Nudes” feature. The stunning Witches of East End star joins fellow celebrity moms Kristen Bell and Minnie Driver for the annual story, which features all of the stars in the buff.

“Chan was really on board with it,” Dewan tells Allure about husband Channing Tatum‘s reaction to the sexy photo shoot. “He knows I’m very comfortable in my own skin.”

Dewan proves it in her latest stripped-down pic, taken by photographer Carter Smith. The petite brunette sizzles with half of her naked body dipped in shallow ocean waters, while the other half glistens above sea level.

Since welcoming her first child, 11-month-old daughter Everly, last May, Dewan has hardly been shy about showing off her amazing post-baby body. The Step Up star, who gained 33 pounds while pregnant with her daughter, remarkably reclaimed her petite size 0 frame in just seven weeks after giving birth! Dewan later told Us Weekly that she “didn’t think much” about losing the baby weight. Part of it was because the lifelong dancer had been vigilant about maintaining her hot body throughout her pregnancy.

“The second I found out I was pregnant, I called [my trainer] Jen Johnson,” Dewan told Us. “We found areas we wanted to target: arms and legs. I wanted to keep the muscles alive. Plus, I felt so much better. Working out kept me sane. I would feel a burst of energy and all my hormones would regulate.”
