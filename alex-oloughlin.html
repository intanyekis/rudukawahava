Alex O’Loughlin is an Australian actor, writer, director, and producer who is best known for playing Lieutenant Commander Steve McGarrett on CBS’ remake of the action police procedural television series Hawaii Five-0 as well as for portraying Cornish fisherman and convict Will Bryant in the 2005 miniseries titled The Incredible Journey of Mary Bryant. Some of his other roles include playing Kevin Hiatt in the FX crime drama series The Shield, Marcus Bohem in the teen supernatural thriller The Invisible, Dr. Andy Yablonski in the CBS medical drama series Three Rivers, Jack Flange in the romantic drama film Oyster Farmer, Mick St. John in the paranormal romance television drama series Moonlight, Marshall Connelly in the musical drama film August Rush, and Stan in the romantic comedy film The Back-up Plan.

Girlfriend / Spouse

Alex O’Loughlin has dated –

  • Holly Valance (2005-2009) – Alex first met actress and singer Holly Valance at their mutual female friend’s house. That particular friend had just moved into a new house and Alex was helping her repair the place and Holly was visiting her. They regularly kept meeting at their friend’s house and eventually started going out. After dating for a couple of months, they moved in together. They went out for about four years, before going separate ways in February 2009 as confirmed by Daily Mail.
  • Amber Clayton (2010-2011) – Alex started dating actress Amber Clayton in 2010 after having grown close while working in the Three Rivers TV series. He owned up to dating her in 2011 but refused to reveal too much as he didn’t want to put his new relationship under the strain of being in the spotlight and also he felt it was no one’s business.
  • Jodhi Meares (2011) – O’Loughlin had a passing affair with model Jodhi Meares in mid-2011. Later, during the GQ Australia Men of the Year Awards in November 2011, he clicked some pictures with her.
  • Malia Jones (2011-Present) – Alex made his relationship with surfer and model Malia Jones public by stepping out together in November 2011 at GQ Australia Men of the Year Awards. In August 2012, Alex’s representative revealed that they had added a baby boy named Lion to their family. Jones already had a son Spike from her earlier relationship. In April 2014, they got married in a secret wedding in Hawaii, where they have been living together.

    Personal Trainer

    Alex started doing karate at a very young age, which helped him establish a solid foundation. Also, in his early 20s, he used to go for heavy lifting exercises. However, he has mellowed down since and instead prefers moderate lifting exercises.

    He works out about 5 times a week and his workouts focus on circuit and interval training. In the circuit, usually, three to five exercises are done one after another without any rest until the circuit is completed.

    He likes to mix up his workout by indulging in surfing in his free time. And, as he lives in Hawaii and his wife was a professional surfer, he gets plenty of opportunities to do his favorite pastime. He also likes to go trail running to unwind and destress.

    He makes sure that he is hydrated and it is his self-imposed rule to drink 2 gallons of water daily. His typical diet plan is like this –

    • Breakfast – Half cup of oatmeal, some almonds, banana, and reduced-fat milk.
    • Lunch and Dinner – A balanced meal comprising of a protein source like chicken breast, fresh vegetables, and healthy carbs such as brown rice and whole pasta.

    Alex O’Loughlin Facts

  • In 2010, BuddyTV placed him on #2 on the list of “TV’s 100 Sexiest Men of 2010”. He bagged the #9 spot in 2011’s list.
  • In his teenage years, he worked as a plumber for about 3 months. He rates it as his worst job.
  • He portrayed the character of William Bryant, a Cornish fisherman and convict who was transported to Australia on the First Fleet, in The Incredible Journey of Mary Bryant. It is 2005 miniseries loosely based on the life of Mary Bryant, an English girl from Cornwall who was transported to the Australian Penal Colony on the First Fleet with other prisoners bound for Botany Bay.
  • For his role in The Incredible Journey of Mary Bryant, he was nominated for an Australian Film Institute Award for “Best Lead Actor in Television” as well as a Logie Award for “Most Outstanding Actor in a Drama Series”.
  • In 2005, he traveled to London to screen test for the iconic role of James Bond. He considers it the biggest screen test, he has done.
  • As a child, he suffered from obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD). He later found out that he suffered from Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) as well.
  • In his free time, he likes to play his guitar.
  • He is proud of his fishing skills. He learned the technique to catch fly-fish, which is known as crack a whip in his childhood.
  • While living in Australia, he was once involved in fighting a bushfire.
