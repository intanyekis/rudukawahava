Ariana Grande's Ex Dalton Gomez Goes Instagram Official With His New Girlfriend

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Highlights

  • Dalton Gomez and Maika Monroe are Instagram official after a few months of dating, sharing romantic photos together.
  • Following his divorce from Ariana Grande, Dalton Gomez seems to have completely moved on with his new relationship.
  • Ariana Grande is also happy in a new relationship with Ethan Slater after her divorce, despite facing backlash.

Just months after his divorce from Ariana Grande, Dalton Gomez seems to be in love all over again. He has been dating actress Maika Monroe for a few months now, but they have been keeping their relationship very private, with no official announcements or anything.

However, it seems they're getting serious now, as they have officially posted about their relationship on Instagram. The actress shared several photos of romantic moments together, even one that included the couple kissing, so it's probably safe to say that the real estate agent has completely moved on.

Maika Monroe Shares Photodump Of Her Romance With Dalton Gomez

It seems Dalton Gomez has recovered nicely from his divorce. The real estate agent and actress Maika Monroe have been rumored to be in a relationship for a few months now, but now they have confirmed their romance by making it Instagram-official.

Maika Monroe made a photodump of what looked like a romantic trip she and Dalton Gomez went on together. The first photo showed the two of them kissing in front of an old house, and then there were several snaps of romantic dinners, one of their legs resting on top of each other, and finally, a picture of them cuddling in a boat.

The couple was first spotted out in public shortly after Dalton Gomez and Ariana Grande finalized their divorce. The singer paid her ex-husband over a million dollars in a settlement and had to pay for his attorney fees, but despite that, the split was amicable in the end. They will sell their Los Angeles house and divide the proceeds, and now there's nothing stopping them from moving on.

"There is still a strong mutual love and respect between Ariana and Dalton and they wanted to make sure everything was done right in lockstep with one another," a source told TMZ after they reached an agreement.

Ariana Grande Is Also Happy In A New Relationship

Despite the divorce, both Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez seem to be doing very well. In fact, they seem happier than ever. The real estate agent and Maika Monroe are clearly building a nice, healthy relationship, and Ariana Grande is happy with Ethan Slater, despite the backlash she received after they started dating.

The singer met the Broadway star when they were making Wicked together, and people believed he was married when they got together, which caused outrage among fans.
