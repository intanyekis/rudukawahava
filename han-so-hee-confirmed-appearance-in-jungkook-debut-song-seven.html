Han So Hee Rumors About Appearance in Jungkook Debut Song &quot;SEVEN&quot; Confirmed!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

South Korean actress Han So Hee has hinted at her involvement in BTS member Jungkook’s highly-anticipated digital solo track titled “Seven.” The news has caused a stir on social media as fans eagerly await the release of the music video.

Although BTS’ management agency has not officially confirmed Han So Hee’s participation, her recent remarks suggest that she will appear in the video as Jungkook’s love interest.

Rumors are now confirmed and circulating that the actress, known for her roles in “Nevertheless” and “My Name,” is both excited and nervous about her role in the music video.

The confirmation of Han So Hee’s involvement reportedly comes from a close acquaintance of the actress whom she met in Paris. This insider revealed that Han So Hee expressed her excitement and nervousness about the upcoming music video release with Jungkook, scheduled for next week:

“She said she is excited and nervous because of the music video release with Jungkook next week.”
